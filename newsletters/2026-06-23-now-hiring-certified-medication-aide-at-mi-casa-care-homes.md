We are excited to share that Mi Casa Care Homes is currently hiring a Certified Medication Aide to join our dedicated team. If you or someone you know has a passion for elder care and wants to make a meaningful difference every day, we would love to hear from you.

### About the Role

As a Certified Medication Aide at Mi Casa Care Homes, you will play a vital role in the daily lives of our residents. You will administer medications safely and accurately, monitor resident health, assist with activities of daily living, and help maintain the warm, respectful environment our residents count on. This is a paid position working alongside a compassionate team of healthcare professionals in a home-like setting.

### What You Will Do

In this role you will safely administer prescribed medications in accordance with state and facility protocols, monitor residents for any changes in condition and report findings to nursing staff, maintain accurate medical documentation in compliance with HIPAA regulations, assist residents with hygiene, mobility, and basic nursing tasks, and support residents with specialized needs including memory care and hospice care.

### What We Are Looking For

We are looking for someone with experience in assisted living, nursing homes, hospitals, or home care environments. You should have a strong understanding of medication administration principles, medical terminology, and senior care needs. Familiarity with EMR or EHR systems is a plus. Most importantly we are looking for someone who brings patience, compassion, and dedication to everything they do.

### Why Mi Casa

At Mi Casa Care Homes we believe in creating an environment where both residents and staff feel valued and supported. Our small, home-like setting means you will build genuine relationships with residents and their families rather than feeling like just another employee in a large facility. We are proud of the culture we have built and we are looking for someone who shares our commitment to dignified, person-centered care.
