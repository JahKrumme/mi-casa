How to Pay

Payment Options

We work with families to find the payment path that fits. Below are the three ways residents typically cover the cost of care at Mi Casa.

Medicaid (HCBS)

Mi Casa accepts Medicaid through Kansas Home and Community Based Services (HCBS) waiver programs.

Long Term Care Insurance

We accept most long-term care insurance plans. If your loved one has an existing policy, we can work with you and the insurer to confirm coverage and handle the billing process.

Private Pay

Families who pay privately can contact us directly for current rates and availability. We're happy to walk you through what's included and answer any questions about the cost of care.

Have questions about payment? Schedule a visit and we'll talk through your options.
