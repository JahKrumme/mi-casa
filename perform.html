Perform at Mi Casa

Share your gift with our residents. We welcome performers, entertainers, and volunteers of all kinds.

We believe that joy, music, and human connection are as important to wellbeing as any medical care. If you're a performer, entertainer, or volunteer who'd like to share your gift with our residents, we'd love to hear from you. Complete the form below and our team will be in touch. What We Welcome

  • Live music and singing
  • Magic and illusion
  • Therapy and companion animals
  • Arts and crafts instruction
  • Storytelling and reading aloud
  • Dance and movement
  • And much more

What to Expect

Our residents enjoy small, intimate performances in a home-like setting. The group is typically small, which makes for a warm and personal experience for everyone in the room.
